Seafood Linguine

Desription

This speedy seafood pasta is ready in just 20 minutes. A satisfying, low-fat recipe which makes the most of frozen seafood and chopped tomatoes

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 400g can chooped tomatoes
  • 1L chicken stock
  • 300g linguine
  • 240g frozen seafood mix
  • Handful of Parsley leaves

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a wok or large frying pan, then cook the onion and garlic over a medium heat for 5 mins until soft. Add the paprika, tomatoes and stock, then bring to the boil.
  2. Turn down the heat to a simmer, stir in the pasta and cook for 7 mins, stirring occasionally to stop the pasta from sticking.
